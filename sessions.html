Special Sessions

ICCIIoT-2018 invites eminent academicians, scientists, researchers, industrialists, technocrats, government representatives, social visionaries and experts from all strata of society to work together for more meaningful and professional outcome. The ICCIIoT team plans to hold special sessions on various areas of expertise, in any of the tracks based on related theme.

The Special Sessions will be chaired by experts from different fields. The presentation of the papers under such Special Sessions will be scheduled at the venue of the conference, on any of the days during the conference.
