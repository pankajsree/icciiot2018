Important Information for Registration

  • The deadline for registration can be seen in the important dates. It is important that at least one author per accepted paper registers on or before the deadline.
  • Authors registration includes the submission of up to seven (7) pages for full paper.
  • For each extra page authors have to pay USD 10 (INR 650).
  • At least one author must register and need to attend the conference to present the paper in person or through Skype.
  • Participants/Attendees/Listeners are also invited.
  • Certificate of presentation shall be issued only if the paper is presented either in person or through SKYPE(if permitted explicitly).
  • M. Tech./B. Tech./MCA students will be considered as Full time Students.
  • Ph. D. Scholars will be considered as Research Scholars.
  • NIT Agartala Students and Co-Authors (provided the main author has registered) of accepted papers will get 10% discount on base rate in the respective category.
  • IAASSE Members must mention their membership number and also attach a copy of the membership card or any other proof of valid membership
  • In no case, registration fee shall be refunded.

Presentation in Absentia

The author(s) NOT from India who intend to submit their work but are unable to attend the conference in absentia may register and present their paper through SKYPE.

If you are a single author and cannot attend the conference, you may choose to attend the conference by paying the registration fee and present your paper through SKYPE. You shall be sent a Certificate of Participation in Absentia, Conference Proceedings link and relevant literature by mail.
